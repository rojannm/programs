How to read this program

If you don’t get to all of it, that’s not detrimental. As many as you can on a day is what I care about.

3x12 means 3 sets of 12 repetitions.

1/2 exercise A and 2/2 exercise B is a superset: do A, then B right after, no rest between.

Anything with an orange demo tag needs Rojan to show you first before you try it.

Tap any exercise to open its log, then tap a cell to edit it. Add entry starts a new row with today’s date. Same as last time repeats your last session with today’s date. While you’re in a row, the WU and L/R weaker chips below it add those notes with one tap.
